Career Coach

Career Coach Work one-on-one with a Dress for Success Twin Cities client in our Career Center. Help draft and review resumes, draft and review cover letters, set up or maintain linked-in profiles, conduct mock-interviews and strategize job searches. Appointments take place Tuesday, Thursdays and Saturdays with a variety of day time, afternoon and some evening slots.

Clothing Sorter

Clothing Sorter Process incoming clothing donations and help to decide what items are appropriate to keep for our boutique. Great for someone with an eye for fashion and a love of keeping up with current trends and styles.

Clothing Organizer

Clothing Organizer A great opportunity for people who love to organize. Hang, organize and place donations on the boutique floor. Help ensure donations look great and are easy to access.

Front Desk Volunteer

Front Desk Volunteer Help to ensure that Dress for Success Twin Cities is a warm and welcoming place to all clients and volunteers. Help run the front desk; answer phones, greet clients and volunteers, and assist with clothing donation drop offs. We ask that Front Desk Volunteers commit to a regular weekly shift of 3-4 hours.

Clothing Sale Volunteer

Clothing Sale Volunteer Assist shoppers in putting together outfits, manage fitting rooms and help keep the floor organized. Clothing sales are held four times a year on Thursdays, Fridays & Saturdays. Shifts are 3 hours long. A great opportunity to invite a friend along or do with a small group.

Clothing Sale Captain

Clothing Sale Captain Work closely with the Volunteer & Communication Coordinator to plan and execute 4 yearly clothing sales. Great for someone who loves project based work and has an interest or experience in retail or visual merchandising as well as event marketing. This is a yearlong commitment.

Career Advocate

Career Advocate Support a woman through our Interview Skills Program, The Goings Places Network by Walmart Foundation. Connect with your mentee via phone or email once a week, for 10 weeks. Encourage and inspire her during her job search.

Professional Intelligence Mentor

Professional Intelligence Mentor Support a woman through our Job Retention Program, Professional Intelligence. Meet twice a month with your mentee, once in person and once remotely to help support them through their work plan. You will provide guidance and support for 8 months. Mentors should have 10 years in their field and management experience.

Professional Intelligence Muse

Professional Intelligence Muse Provide encouragement to a women participating in our Job Retention Program, Professional Intelligence. Mail an encouraging note or card each week as she completes 6 months of classroom learning. Great for someone who loves quotes and providing inspiration!

Community Ambassador

Community Ambassador Represent Dress for Success Twin Cities at community events designed to increase our awareness. Help to spread the message of Dress for Success Twin Cities to potential clients and volunteers. This is a longer volunteer commitment.

Client Event Set Up Volunteer

Client Event Set Up Volunteer Help to prepare for client events and meetings. Help to prepare name tags, food, arrange the space, greet clients and more. Perfect for someone who loves to host and entertain! Many client events take place on weekday evenings.

Event Photographer

Event Photographer Photograph graduation ceremonies for two of our programs, providing documentation and a special keepsake for our clients. We host four Interview skills graduations that take place during the week day and two Job Retention graduations that take place during the weekends. Dates are TBD.

Graphic Designer

Graphic Designer Help to re-imagine and redesign the clothing sale visual identity. Materials to be updated include price signs, fliers and more.
